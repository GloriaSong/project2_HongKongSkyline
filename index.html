Introduction
Hong Kong Skyline has been recognized as one of the best in the world, with surrounding mountains and Victoria Harbour. Every night, skyscrapers on both sides of the Victoria Habour light up in the show called A Symphony of Lights, which is the largest permanent light and sound festival in the world.
